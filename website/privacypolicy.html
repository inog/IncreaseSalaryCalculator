Privacy Policy for the IncreaseSalaryCalculator App

This privacy policy applies to the IncreaseSalaryCalculator app, developed by Ingo Reschke.

The IncreaseSalaryCalculator app does not collect any personal data from its users. However, the app does use the Google AdMob SDK to display ads. These ads may collect personal data from users, such as their location, interests, and browsing history.

Users can control their privacy settings for Google AdMob ads by visiting the Google AdMob website. Users can choose to opt out of personalized ads, which will prevent Google from collecting personal data for the purpose of displaying ads.

Google will retain personal data collected through the Google AdMob SDK for as long as necessary to provide the services requested by the user. Users can request that Google delete their personal data by visiting the Google AdMob website.

Google may share personal data collected through the Google AdMob SDK with third-party advertisers. Users can learn more about how Google shares data with third parties by visiting the Google Privacy Policy.

Google takes steps to protect the security of personal data collected through the Google AdMob SDK. These steps include using encryption and other security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Google may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users should check this page periodically for the latest information about Google's privacy practices.
